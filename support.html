FAQ

Why does the app ask for Camera access?
The overlay is drawn on top of the live camera preview to help you align cuts. We do not store or upload images.

Why do I see an ATT (tracking) prompt?
The app uses AdMob. We show the App Tracking Transparency prompt where applicable and respect your choice. Ads may be limited if you opt out.

How do I change the slice color or thickness?
Open Settings (gear icon) to adjust circle size, line width, and color, then return to the camera.
